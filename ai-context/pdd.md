Product Definition Document (PDD)
Version 1.0
Product Name
MatriMitra Agency Ecosystem

1. Executive Summary
MatriMitra Agency Ecosystem is a cloud-based platform designed for matrimonial agencies to digitize their operations, manage client profiles, collaborate with trusted agencies, and facilitate successful matrimonial introductions.
Unlike public matrimonial portals, MatriMitra follows an Agency-First model where agencies remain custodians of client relationships while leveraging technology, trust, and intelligence to improve matchmaking outcomes.
The platform acts as a secure operating system for matrimonial agencies rather than a public matchmaking marketplace.

2. Problem Statement
Traditional matrimonial agencies face several challenges:
