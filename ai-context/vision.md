# MatriMitra Vision

## Product Vision

MatriMitra is a SaaS platform designed specifically for matrimonial agencies.

Unlike public matrimonial websites, MatriMitra enables agencies to remain at the center of the matchmaking process while providing modern tools for client management, secure collaboration, trust building, and relationship tracking.

The platform acts as an Agency Operating System that helps agencies digitize their business, collaborate with other agencies, and improve matchmaking outcomes.

---

## Mission

To become the most trusted operating system for matrimonial agencies globally.

---

## Core Principles

### Agency First

Agencies are the primary customers and primary users.

### Trust By Design

Every profile access, proposal, and interaction is traceable.

### Controlled Collaboration

Agencies can collaborate without losing ownership of their clients.

### Privacy First

Profiles are not publicly searchable.

### Family Centric

Families remain an important part of the matchmaking journey.
